- VSS is the digital ground, VSSA is the Analog ground (DAC/ADC)

-VDD is +3.3V for the MCU. VBAT is something else with an external battery but we can use +3.3v for it.

-Decoupling capacitors are used to place small energy storages along the MCU so that it doesn't rely on main battery.
    - 1 Decoupling-capa(100n F) per VBAT and VDD. Extra 10 uF at the end (volt decoupling)
